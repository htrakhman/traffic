## H2: The MUTCD formula everyone uses

- Taper length `L` calculation:
  - Speed ≤ 40 mph: `L = WS²/60`
  - Speed > 40 mph: `L = WS`
  - Where W = lane width in ft, S = speed limit in mph
- Cone spacing in the taper: 1 per every `L / 10` feet (minimum 10 cones)
- Cone spacing in the buffer + activity area: 2× taper spacing

## H2: Worked example — typical NJ 40 mph road closure

- 12 ft lane, 40 mph → `L = 12 × 40² / 60 = 320 ft`
- Cone spacing in taper: `320 / 10 = 32 ft` (round to 40 ft / per MUTCD min)
- Taper cones: 10
- 100 ft buffer + 500 ft work zone = 600 ft at 80 ft spacing = 8 cones
- **Total: ~18 cones, plus spares (always bring 25%) = ~23 cones**

## H2: When you need more than the minimum

- Nighttime work: use reflective 36" cones, tighter spacing
- Adjacent pedestrian traffic: add channelizing devices between cones
- Curves: tighter spacing through the curve
- High-volume roads: double up first/last cones in taper
