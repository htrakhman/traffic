2. Verify it's the right NJ business (check location + recent posts)
3. Click **Message**
4. Copy the block below for that lead — **send as-is, no editing needed**
5. Mark it ✅ in the tracking table at the bottom + paste the FB page URL

Don't blast all 18 in 20 minutes. Send 3–4, take a 15–30 min break,
repeat. Mimics human behavior, stays safe.

## Framing reminder (Austen's insight)

cushions / short-notice MUTCD packages** — NOT cones. Contractors
already own cones. Save those for the follow-up once engaged.
